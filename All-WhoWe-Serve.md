**Q1: I'm struggling in school. Will EduMeUp actually help me?**
A: Yes, but first we need to understand WHY you're struggling. Take the free diagnostic - it identifies exact gaps (often from years ago). We fix those gaps FIRST, then current content becomes easier. 91% of struggling students who complete our remediation pass their exams.

A: Recommended: 60-90 min daily (20-30 min per subject). But it's flexible - study more on weekends, less on busy days. Progress based on mastery, not hours.

A: Multiple support options: (1) Re-watch lesson, (2) Try different explanation in AI chatbot, (3) Ask human tutor for 1-hour session ($15-35). Most concepts click within 2-3 attempts.

**Q7: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Yes. Learn on phone, tablet, or computer. Download lessons for offline study. Progress syncs automatically across devices.

**Q8: What's the difference between Self-Learning and Teacher-Guided?**
A: Self-Learning = you study independently with AI support. Teacher-Guided = same content PLUS weekly live sessions with human teacher, homework review, progress monitoring. Choose based on how much structure you need.

**Q9: Can I get a certificate?**
A: Yes. Certificate of Completion issued for each program/grade level completed. Shows mastery percentage. Not equivalent to Cambridge certificate (that requires sitting actual exams), but demonstrates learning achievement.
